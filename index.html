ArcTimers Legal & Privacy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

ArcTimers (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a fan-made ARC Raiders companion published by Franck Clement. It is an independent project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Embark Studios AB or ARC Raiders. This document covers the Terms of Use and Privacy Notice for the ArcTimers iOS application.

2. What ArcTimers Does

  • Displays live and upcoming ARC Raiders event schedules using public community feeds and MetaForge data.
  • Lets you pin events and configure custom notification scheduling so you never miss a Night Raid or Matriarch rotation.
  • Shows weekly Trials fetched from MetaForge feeds.

The information is provided “as is.” Embark Studios and ARC Raiders are not affiliated with this app.

3. Eligibility & Usage

The app is intended for players aged 13+. You do not need an account to use ArcTimers. By installing, you agree to follow these terms, comply with all laws, and respect the intellectual property of Embark Studios and other licensors.

4. Data We Collect

  • Local Data Only: Event pins, tracked IDs, notification rules, and widget preferences stay on your device (and in iCloud if you sync app data).
  • Notifications: The app stores the times you choose for alerts so it can schedule them with Apple’s notification APIs.
  • Purchases: If you subscribe to ArcTimers Pro, Apple handles the transaction and provides an anonymized receipt ID.

We do not collect personally identifiable information, contacts, photos, GPS data, or usage analytics.

5. How We Use Data

  • Local Storage: Data stays on your device to power widgets, Live Activities, watch complications, etc.
  • Notifications: We use Apple’s UNUserNotificationCenter to deliver reminders and never share those schedules.
  • Subscriptions: Apple’s App Store frameworks validate purchases and restore entitlements.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your info. We only share data when:

  • Processing payments via Apple.
  • Required by law or to enforce legal rights.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You control everything stored locally. Delete the app or use the in-app reset to erase schedules and trials. For purchase history questions or data deletion requests, email arctimers@gmail.com with “ArcTimers Data Request”.

8. Children’s Privacy

ArcTimers is designed for ARC Raiders fans 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 and encourage guardians to review device permissions.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this page to reflect new features or additional locales. When that happens we’ll update the date above and summarize the changes in the app release notes.

10. Contact

For privacy or legal questions email arctimers@gmail.com. For support issues, include your device model, iOS version, and the steps you took so we can reproduce the issue.
